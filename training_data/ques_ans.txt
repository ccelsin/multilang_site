What are the staple foods for a balanced diet?
Staple foods include fruits, vegetables, proteins (such as meat, fish, and legumes), whole grains, and dairy products or their alternatives.
What is the importance of fiber in the diet?
Fiber aids digestion, regulates bowel movements, and can help control blood sugar and cholesterol levels.
How many meals should I eat per day?
It is generally recommended to eat three main meals and two snacks, but this can vary based on individual needs.
What is a healthy serving of fruits per day?
It is recommended to consume about 2 to 3 servings of fruits per day.
